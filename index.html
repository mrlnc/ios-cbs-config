iOS Cell Broadcast Configurations

Cell Broadcast configuration as found in iOS. iOS has "Carrier Bundles" and "Country Bundles" with network-specific or country-specific configuration. The list may not be complete or correct - it's auto-generated from the carrier.plist files I found in OTA updates and on itunes.com/version, but carriers may have other channels to distribute their configs.

The config here is also incomplete: networks could potentially send messages on multiple IDs in parallel (say, 919 and 4370) but only configure a single channel for iOS.

    "AlertConfigurations": {
